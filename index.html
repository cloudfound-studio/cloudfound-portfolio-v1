How a project usually works

Every project is slightly different, but here’s the general flow so you know what to expect.

01

Quick intro call

We talk for 20–30 minutes about your business, what’s not working with your current site (or that you don’t have one), and what “good” would look like.

02

Design & build

I put together a simple structure, visuals, and copy based on what we discussed, then refine it with your feedback until it feels right.

03

Launch & care

Once your site goes live, I can either hand it off to you or continue to look after updates, backups, and small content changes on an ongoing care plan.
